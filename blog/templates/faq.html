General Questions

What is Classics On Screen?

Classics On Screen is a platform dedicated to bringing classic films back to the big screen. Take a look at our About page for more information.

How do I find upcoming screenings?

You can find all our upcoming screenings on the Home page. As people create new screenings, they'll appear there automatically.

Why do new shows have to be at least 3 weeks in the future?

There are two main reasons for this: the first is that the venue has to plan their own programme and need to have time to work around your show if need be. The second is that, for cinemas at least, films are not available "on-demand" and booking new films tends to be manual, slow and time-consuming.

Tickets and Booking

How do I purchase tickets?

You can buy credits via your profile page and use these to buy tickets for a particular show or create one of your own!

What is your refund policy?

Typically, we arrange for cinema tickets for regular shows (not including special events) at your local cinema rather than issuing refunds. However, feel free to reach out via the Contact Us page.

Why credits?

Here's the thing, we want to keep things as simple as possible. Due to the costs involved, we also need to keep refunds and other cash transfers to a minimum. A credits system allows you to use your ticket anywhere without having to worry about having to top up the difference in ticket prices or have a stray 50p sitting in your account.

I've seen films at a particular cinema cheaper than the credits you have here. Why is that?

It's partly down us being able to handle multiple sites with different ticket prices, it's partly because we don't want to have to use booking fees and it's partly down to the fact that one-off shows cost more to put on (see our About page for more on that last bit).

Other Questions

How can I suggest a film?

You can suggest films through our Contact Us page. We appreciate your suggestions!
