Our Story

We started Classics On Screen with a simple mission: to bring classic films back to the big screen for everyone to enjoy. We believe that these timeless stories deserve to be experienced in a communal setting, with the full cinematic experience.

Our team is made up of passionate film enthusiasts who are dedicated to curating a diverse selection of classic movies, from beloved classics to hidden gems. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for film lovers of all ages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate the art of filmmaking and to provide a platform for people to connect with these iconic films. We aim to foster a sense of community among film lovers and to create memorable experiences that will last a lifetime.

  • To showcase a wide range of classic films from different genres and eras.
  • To provide affordable and accessible screenings for everyone.
  • To create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all film lovers.
  • To promote film education and appreciation.
