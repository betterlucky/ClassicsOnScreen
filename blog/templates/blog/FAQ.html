General Questions

What is Classics On Screen?

Classics On Screen is a platform dedicated to bringing classic films back to the big screen. We curate a selection of timeless movies and organize screenings at various locations.

How do I find upcoming screenings?

You can find all our upcoming screenings on the Home page. We regularly update the schedule with new events.

Tickets and Booking

How do I purchase tickets?

Ticket purchasing information will be available on the event page for each screening. We usually provide links to online ticketing platforms or information about purchasing tickets at the venue.

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy varies depending on the event and ticketing platform. Please refer to the specific event page or contact us for more details.

Other Questions

How can I suggest a film?

You can suggest films through our Contact Us page. We appreciate your suggestions!
